City Planning Commission

Overview

The City of South Fulton City Planning Commission oversees all zoning and planning of the City

Members

The Commission consists of eight members who are residents of the city. One member was nominated by each member of the City Council and the Mayor. The Planning Commission member shall serve a term consistent with that of the member of the City Council (or the Mayor) making the nomination.
